Our Story

Leaf & Bean began with a simple idea: create a calm, welcoming space where great coffee and community can thrive — without costing the earth. We source ethically, reduce waste, and design every detail to feel warm, natural, and refreshingly simple.

  • Compostable packaging & reusable cup program
  • Direct‑trade beans from smallholder farms
  • Locally baked goods and seasonal produce
